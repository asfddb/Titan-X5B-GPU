# Titan X5-B GPU — LLP Registration Guide

This guide outlines the process to legally incorporate your GPU IP business in India as a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP).

## Why an LLP?
An LLP protects your personal assets, looks professional to B2B enterprise buyers, and is much cheaper to maintain than a Private Limited Company. It also qualifies for the Startup India initiative.

---

## 1. Pick a Name
You must select a unique name. Here are 5 candidate names likely available on the MCA (Ministry of Corporate Affairs) portal:
1. **Adhiraj Silicon LLP**
2. **Titan GPU Systems LLP**
3. **Astra Semiconductor LLP**
4. **X5B Semiconductor LLP**
5. **Open Silicon India LLP**

*Action: Go to the MCA portal (mca.gov.in) -> MCA Services -> Check Company Name to verify availability.*

## 2. Obtain a Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
You will need a Class 3 DSC to sign documents digitally on the MCA portal.
**Providers:**
- eMudhra
- VSign
- Capricorn Identity Services

*Cost: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500*

## 3. Name Reservation (RUN-LLP)
Log in to the MCA V3 portal and use the **RUN-LLP (Reserve Unique Name)** service. Submit 2 name choices. Once approved, the name is reserved for 3 months.

## 4. Incorporation Filing (FiLLiP)
After the name is reserved, file the **FiLLiP (Form for incorporation of Limited Liability Partnership)** form.
This single form covers:
- Incorporation application
- DPIN (Designated Partner Identification Number) allotment
- PAN (Permanent Account Number) application
- TAN (Tax Deduction Account Number) application

*Documents needed:* Proof of registered office address (utility bill), NOC from landlord, ID/Address proof of partners (PAN and Aadhaar).

## 5. Draft and File the LLP Agreement
Within 30 days of incorporation, you must draft your LLP Agreement on non-judicial stamp paper (stamp duty varies by state) and file **Form 3** on the MCA portal.
*Note: You can find standard LLP agreement templates online or consult a CS (Company Secretary).*

## 6. Open a Current Bank Account
Once you receive the Certificate of Incorporation and PAN, visit your preferred bank (e.g., HDFC, ICICI, SBI) to open a Current Account in the name of the LLP.

## 7. GST Registration (Optional initially, but recommended)
If you expect B2B transactions with foreign clients or exceed the turnover threshold, apply for GST registration on the GST portal (gst.gov.in).

## 8. Apply for Startup India Recognition
Register on the Startup India portal (startupindia.gov.in) using your LLP details. This gives you tax exemptions, easier compliance, and eligibility for grants (like the DLI scheme).

---

**Estimated Cost:** ₹10,000 - ₹15,000 (if using a CA/CS service)
**Estimated Time:** 5-7 working days
