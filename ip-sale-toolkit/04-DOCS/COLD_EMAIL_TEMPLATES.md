## Sending Strategy

### Week-by-Week Plan

**Week 1 (Days 57-63 in roadmap):**
- Send 50 emails total (10 per day Mon-Fri)
- Track every email in CRM: company, contact, date, status

**Week 2 (Days 64-70):**
- Follow-up emails to non-responders (Templates 2 and 3)
- Take 5-10 sales calls
- Send NDAs to interested parties

**Week 3 (Days 71-77):**
- Negotiate first eval license
- Cold-email 30 more companies (80 total contacted)

**Week 4 (Days 78-84):**
- Sign first eval license
- Continue cold outreach (target: 100 companies contacted by Day 90)

### Realistic Response Rates

| Stage | Rate |
|-------|------|
| Cold email sent | 100% |
| Cold email opened | ~50% |
| Cold email replied | ~5-10% |
| Reply → call booked | ~50% |
| Call → NDA signed | ~30% |
| NDA → eval license | ~50% |
| Eval → commercial license | ~20% |

**So:** 100 cold emails → 7 replies → 3 calls → 1 NDA → 0.5 eval → 0.1 commercial license

Translation: You need to send **1000 emails** to get **1 commercial
license**. That's the reality of B2B IP sales.

But each email takes 5-10 minutes. 1000 emails = 100 hours = ~3
months of part-time outreach. That's exactly what Month 3 is for.
