### Slide 2: The Problem
*   **The AI Compute Bottleneck:** Current GPUs and clusters are hitting physical and networking limits.
*   **Interconnect Latency:** Moving data between thousands of discrete GPUs is slow, power-hungry, and expensive.
*   **The Cost of Scale:** Training next-generation frontier models (GPT-5, AGI-level models) requires exponential increases in compute, making traditional data center scaling economically unviable.
*   **Power Wall:** Traditional architectures are too power-inefficient to sustain the AI revolution.

---

### Slide 3: The Solution - Wafer-Scale Integration
*   **The Titan X5 Approach:** Instead of slicing silicon into small chips, we build an entire system on a single, massive wafer.
*   **Zero Off-Chip Latency:** By keeping data on the wafer, we eliminate slow, power-hungry network hops between discrete GPUs.
*   **Orders of Magnitude Improvement:** 100x bandwidth, 10x lower latency, and dramatically lower power per FLOP compared to traditional clusters.
*   **A Data Center in a Box:** Replacing racks of servers, miles of networking cables, and complex distributed software with a single unified compute engine.

*   **Market Desperation:** AI labs, hyperscalers, and sovereign AI initiatives are severely compute-constrained and actively seeking leapfrog hardware solutions.
